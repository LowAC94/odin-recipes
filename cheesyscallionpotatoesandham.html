Cheesy Scallion Potatoes and Ham

Description

The best bubbly, creamy, cheesy casserole of tender scalloped potatoes and ham.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 small yellow onion
  • 3 tablespoons of all-pupose flour
  • 1 cup of half-and-half
  • 1/2 cup of whole milk
  • Salt and ground pepper to tatse
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon worcestershire sauce
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 4 large russet potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped of hickory smoked ham
  • 1/2 cup grated cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated monterary jack cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 deegrees F (190 degrees C). Butter a baking dish
  2. Melt butter in a large skillet over meiudm heat.Add onion; cook and stir until starting to soften, 3 to 4 minutes. Sprinkle flour over and whisk together. Cook until golden brown, until golden brown, about 2 minutes. Stir in half-and-half; whisk until thickened, 3 to 4 minutes. Remove from heat and season sauce with salt and pepper. Add garlic powder,woresterschire sauce,onion powder and paprika.
  3. Place a half sliced potatoes into the baking dish; top with half the chopped ham. Sprinkle half of the cheddar cheese and half the moterery jack cheese on top. Pour in half the sauce from the skillet. Repeat layering the remaining potatoes, ham, cheese, and sauce. Ending with a layer of cheese and sauce. Cover dish with aluminum foil.
  4. Bake in preheated oven for 40 minutes. Remove the foil and bake until cheese on top is golden and sauce is bubbling, 20 to 30 minutes more.
